Ministik Five Meadows to Waskahegan Loop Hike

May 29 2024·96 words·1 min
Ministik Hiking Geocaching

I undertook a 14.7 km “grand loop” hike in the Ministik today, what I call the “Five Meadows Hike”. It was a gloriously beautiful day to do it too.

 

 

 

 

When I started the hike at the UofA Access Road gate a constant swarm of small blue-ish dragonflies accompanied me down the trail. You may have to look closely to see them.
